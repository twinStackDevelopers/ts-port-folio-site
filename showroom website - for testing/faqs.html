Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Velocity Motors unique?

Velocity Motors redefines luxury automotive retail through:

  • Exclusive Selection: Curated inventory of premium vehicles
  • White-Glove Service: Personalized attention from start to finish
  • Ownership Benefits: Complimentary maintenance and concierge services
  • Transparent Process: No hidden fees or pressure tactics

How does your trade-in process work?

Our trade-in process is designed for convenience:

  1. Online Valuation: Get an instant estimate on our website
  2. In-Person Appraisal: Schedule a complimentary vehicle inspection
  3. Competitive Offer: Receive a market-based purchase offer
  4. Seamless Application: Apply trade value directly to your new purchase

We accept trade-ins regardless of where you purchased your current vehicle.

What financing options are available?

We offer flexible financing solutions:

Retail Financing

  • Competitive rates from top national lenders
  • Terms from 24 to 84 months
  • Quick approval process

Lease Programs

  • Low monthly payments
  • Multiple annual mileage options
  • Purchase options at lease end

What's included in your maintenance package?

Our comprehensive package includes:

  • Scheduled Maintenance: Factory-recommended services
  • Fluid Services: Oil changes, coolant, and brake fluid
  • Tire Care: Rotations, pressure checks, and alignments
  • Roadside Assistance: 24/7 support anywhere in North America
  • Concierge Benefits: Loaner vehicles and pickup/delivery

How do I schedule a service appointment?

Multiple convenient options:

  • Online: Via our customer portal with real-time availability
  • Mobile App: Schedule and manage appointments on-the-go
  • Phone: Call our service department directly
  • In-Person: Visit our state-of-the-art service center

Same-day appointments often available for urgent needs.
