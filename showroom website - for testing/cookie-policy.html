How we use cookies to enhance your experience

Our Cookie Policy

This policy explains how Velocity Motors uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience across our digital platforms.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites remember information about your visit.

Essential Cookies

These necessary cookies enable core functionality:

  • Session maintenance for secure areas of our site
  • Shopping cart and transaction processing
  • User authentication and security features

Performance Cookies

These help us understand how visitors interact with our site:

  • Google Analytics for traffic measurement
  • Error tracking to improve stability
  • Load balancing for optimal performance

Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage cookie settings through your browser controls. Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Third-Party Cookies

We partner with trusted services that may set cookies:

  • Advertising networks for relevant vehicle offers
  • Social media platforms for sharing capabilities
  • Payment processors for secure transactions

Cookie Duration

Cookies may be either:

  • Session cookies: Temporary, deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain until expiration or manual deletion

Policy Updates

We may update this policy as technology evolves. Significant changes will be noted on our website.
