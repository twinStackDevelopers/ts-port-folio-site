Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us at Velocity Motors

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Velocity Motors, we prioritize the protection of your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy outlines our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection.

Transparency Matters

We believe you should always know what data we collect, how it's used, and how you can control it.

Information Collection

We collect information to provide better services to all our customers:

  • Personal Details: Name, contact information, and identification when you engage with our services
  • Vehicle Preferences: Your interests and purchase history to personalize your experience
  • Financial Information: Only when necessary for financing applications
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and usage patterns for website improvement

How We Use Your Information

Your data enables us to deliver exceptional service:

  • Process transactions and maintain service records
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Improve our products, services, and digital platforms
  • Communicate important updates and offers (with consent)
  • Comply with legal requirements and prevent fraud

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact our privacy team to exercise these rights.

Data Security Measures

We implement robust security protocols including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Comprehensive employee training on data protection

Policy Updates

We may periodically update this policy. Significant changes will be communicated through our website or direct notification.
